# Consolidate Mode Skill

When we are working in "consolidate" mode, we are still focusing on files in the ./notes/ directory. Instead of just dictating notes (like in "sort mode"), we are reviewing existing notes, and choosing to refine them.

I will be asking you to focus on certain ideas within the notes.

## When to Use

- Use this skill when my prompt begines with "CONSOLIDATE" or "consolidate:"; or when I explicitly load the "consolidate-mode" skill

## Instructions

- First look at "OPEN QUESTION" questions, and try to resolve them.
- We may re-order chunks, combine chunks, and/or split chunks. We will be look at chunks that have overlapping ideas, and choose how to resolve them.
- You can propose a consolidate to me, then I will give you permission to proceed with the change.
